Red velvet cookies

A distinctive cookie dish that combines eggs and other ingredients to produce this unique dish that can be served on special occasions

Preparation time

  • Total

    : Approximately 1 hour

  • Preparation

    : 30 minutes

  • Cooking

    : 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups of fine Kuwaiti flour
  • 200 grams of Lurpak butter
  • Half a cup of white sugar
  • Half a cup of brown sugar, moist
  • Vanilla flavour
  • A sprinkle of sea salt
  • And Belgian chocolate 250 grams
  • Red color
